Description

The ethnicity of a patient, as specified by the patient. This valueset is comprised of codes from NHS Data Dictionary: Ethnic Category Code.

Reference: http://www.datadictionary.nhs.uk/data_dictionary/attributes/e/end/ethnic_category_code_de.asp?shownav=0.
